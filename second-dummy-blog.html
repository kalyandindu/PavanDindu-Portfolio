    My expectations and reality from college and MNC company.


    My initial expectation going into a university is to have Good lectures, Library, Practicle exposure, Good Placement.But in reality majority of the faculty and not on par and also curriculum doesn't suits the current industy requirments with lack in practical exposure.

    Life in MNC, Please don't expect any devlopment stuff as majority of the projects will be service oriented which requires fresher where work wont be exiting, challanging and nowhere related to current market trends. Better way to escape MNS cycle is to learn good skills and try getting into startup's or product based and your career will be sky rocketed with good learning scope and payscale.

    Expectation and reality in both gradution and MNC job were entirely different, For good carrer, Get good technicle skills and get placed in startups and product based companies. Where your work will be more valued, You will learn more, Will acquire industry relevant technical skills and at the end life will be much exciting.
