    Introduction

    Indian Premier League(IPL),one of the world's most popular cricketing and sport league. It is the one of the top most viewed, Revenue generated sport event in the world. It was founded in the year 2007 by BCCI as an initiative to provide a platform for new budding cricketer to learn from the best in industry and also to generate revenue which inturn can be used to raise the standards of domestic Indian cricket


    Team structure and prize money

    It includes 8 cricketing teams playing for trophy with four foreign and seven indian players featuring in each team which follows double round-robin and playoffs format. Squad strength should be in between 18 to 25 players and the salary cap for each franchise is 85 crores.Prize money of 50 crores will be distributrd among top three accordingly. In aditional to that best performers in each match are given additional prize money.

    Pros

    Best players from accross the nations feature in this tournament. As a result all the youngsters get a chance to share the dressing room and learn from them and all the current superstars of indian team are the gifts from this tournament.

    India being a avid cricket loving nation, IPL provides good wholesome entertainment to all the cricket lovers. Even in the hard covid times IPL provided cricketing lovers something to cheer for. With cricket player earning good income, Revenue fro IPL is being used to develop the cricket infrastructure accross the nation thus encouraging new talents to opt this beautiful game as a career.


    controversies

    Game also involved in few controversies of spot fixing, criticism across sposerships etc.. All the current talent indian cricketing stars have used this platform to improve technicalities around the game and emerge as best in the game.

    conclusion

    IPL is the most loved and celebrated cricket tournment. It may continues to bring more smiles and gets bigger with each passing year.
